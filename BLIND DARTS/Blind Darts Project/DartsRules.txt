The player(s) will be playing against a random number selection representing the sqaces on a dart board.
On Mode Selection Player 1 can choose to go against a CPU or another user.
When the game begins players will be given 3 darts to "blindly throw" each turn
Once a dart is thrown in it will choose a random number representing a "space" on the board.
The number chosen will deduct the points of the current player. This process is repeated until there are no more darts.
When a player runs out of darts, their turn is over will change to the second player or CPU.
When a player's points reach 0 they will be the winner and the game will end.
